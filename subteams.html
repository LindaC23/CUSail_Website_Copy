Sub Teams

Mechanical

The Mechanical team is responsible for designing and manufacturing all of the mechanical components of the boat. We also make sure that the boat is sealed and waterproof before testing. Our team members work on individual tasks like building a new windvane, or redesigning the deck to better contain the electronics and motor control and incorporate more reliable waterproofing. Working on the mechanical team involves constant problem solving and hands-on as well as technical skills.

Navigation

The Navigation sub-team deals with all electronics and software that gives Sail-Vane autonomy. Sail-Vaneâ€™s autonomous capabilities rest on two major pillars: gathering data from its environment and, making and executing reasonable decisions given the gathered data. An array of sensors allow detection of global position, wind direction and boat direction. These parameters are then used to determine the optimal position of the sail and tail-vane. The Navigation team is responsible for implementing these sensors to collect usable and accurate data, devising an algorithm to allow Sail-Vane to navigategiven the collected data, and creating additional sub-systems that allow for communication with Sail-Vane, data logging and efficient power distribution to all sub-systems

Business

The Business team is a multi-disciplinary team that manages all business aspects of the team. We write and manage the budget for the team, and make all purchases. The Business team is also responsible for working to obtain sponsorship from corporations and individuals. We also design the team website and are responsible for all adminstrative team tasks.
