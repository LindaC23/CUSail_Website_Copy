About Us

CUSail is an engineering project team that designs and manufactures an autonomous sailboat. CUSail offers students across many disciplines the opportunity to combine cutting-edge technology with advanced mechanical design to engineer a smarter and faster boat every year.

SailBot

Each year in June, CUSail competes in the SailBot International Robotic Sailing Regatta to help the team evaluate the boat’s design and figure out what to improve on in future iterations.

Long Term Goals

Sail the Length of Cayuga Lake

Our first goal is to sail from the southern end to the northern end of Cayuga Lake. Cayuga Lake is just under 40 miles long, so we would be able to test our navigation algorithm on a larger scale.

Empower Oceanic Research

After we perfect or mechanical design and navigation algorithm, we hope to experiment with applications of our boat for oceanic research. This would unlock a world of research opportunities, from monitoring weather trends to tracking whale migration patterns.
