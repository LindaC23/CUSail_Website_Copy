Join Our Team

We are looking for passionate, talented, and enthusiastic students looking to gain valuable learning experiences through the application of classroom principles. Our team members are passionate about engineering and design, are willing to work hard to achieve our goals, and enjoy working together as a team.

  • We are looking for Mechanical, Navigation, and Business team members of all years and majors
  • No prior engineering experience needed
  • An enthusiasm for exploring new ideas is required!
  • CUSail is often taken for 1-3 credits (MAE 1900 or MAE 4900)
