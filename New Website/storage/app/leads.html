About Us

CUSail is a Cornell Engineering Project Team that works together as a team to build a fully autonomous robotic sailboat for competition and oceanic research. We hope that from our experiences in the competition we can achieve our long term goal of finding an optimal boat design to create a fleet of autonomous vessels.
