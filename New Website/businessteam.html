Business

The Business Subteam is a multi-disciplinary team that manages all business aspects of the team. We create the team's budget and manage team finances. The Business Subteam also works to obtain all sponsorships from corporations and individuals. We design the team website, brand, and merchandise in addition to being responsible for all adminstrative team tasks.

About Us

CUSail is a Cornell Engineering Project Team that works together as a team to build a fully autonomous robotic sailboat for competition and oceanic research. We hope that from our experiences in the competition we can achieve our long term goal of finding an optimal boat design to create a fleet of autonomous vessels.
