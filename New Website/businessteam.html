Business

The Business Subeam is a multi-disciplinary team that manages all business aspects of the team. We create the team's budget, manage team finances, and make all purchases. The Business team is also responsible for obtaining all sponsorships from corporations and individuals. We design the team website, brand, and merchandise in addition to being responsible for all adminstrative team tasks.
