Navigation

The Navigation Subteam deals with all electronics and software that gives Sail-Vane autonomy. Sail-Vane’s autonomous capabilities rest on two major pillars: gathering data from its environment and, making and executing reasonable decisions given the gathered data. An array of sensors allow detection of global position, wind direction and boat direction. These parameters are then used to determine the optimal position of the sail and tail-vane. The Navigation team is responsible for implementing these sensors to collect usable and accurate data, devising an algorithm to allow Sail-Vane to navigategiven the collected data, and creating additional sub-systems that allow for communication with Sail-Vane, data logging and efficient power distribution to all sub-systems
