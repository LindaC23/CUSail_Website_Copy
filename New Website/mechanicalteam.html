Mechanical

The Mechanical Subteam is responsible for designing and manufacturing all of the mechanical components of the boat. We also make sure that the boat is sealed and waterproof before testing. Our team members work on individual tasks like building a new windvane, or redesigning the deck to better contain the electronics and motor control and incorporate more reliable waterproofing. Working on the mechanical team involves constant problem solving and hands-on as well as technical skills.

About Us

CUSail is a Cornell Engineering Project Team that works together as a team to build a fully autonomous robotic sailboat for competition and oceanic research. We hope that from our experiences in the competition we can achieve our long term goal of finding an optimal boat design to create a fleet of autonomous vessels.
