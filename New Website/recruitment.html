General Questions

Do I need any experience to join CUSail?

No! We look for people who are passionate and eager to learn. We teach everyone everything they need to know to succeed on our team, and we want people who will be enthusiastic and open to learning.

How many credits do people usually take CUSail for?

Most people on our team take CUSail for 3 credits. Students can enroll in MAE 4900 (non-freshmen), ENGRG 1400 (freshmen), or ENGRG 3400. Most freshmen or members in their first semester on the team take it for 1 credit.
